Creamy Butter Linguini with Asparagus & Veggies

A bright and comforting dish, loaded with greens and wrapped in a rich, buttery sauce—perfect for family dinners (kid-approved!).

Fresh Asparagus

Green Peas

Zucchini

Baby Spinach

Lemon

Pecorino Romano

Unsalted Butter

Garlic Cloves

Linguini

Step 1: Boil linguini until al dente. In the last 2 minutes, toss in asparagus and peas. Drain and reserve ½ cup pasta water.

Step 2: Sauté garlic and zucchini in butter and olive oil until tender.

Step 3: Add cream, cheese, and lemon zest to the skillet. Simmer and season to taste.

Step 4: Toss in pasta, veggies, and spinach. Stir gently to coat, adding pasta water as needed.

Creamy, comforting, and packed with fresh veggies, this dish is a tasty way to sneak greens onto the table. Even picky eaters will love twirling buttery pasta strands—and you’ll love how easy it is to make!
